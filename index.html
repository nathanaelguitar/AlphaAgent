Our Story

Born in a post-op ER, built for the future of medicine

AlphaAgent started in an ER waiting room. After an emergency finger surgery following a spider bite, our founder—an Analytics Engineer at HCA Healthcare—watched as understaffed surgical teams spent more time wrestling with Epic and Cerner than attending to patients.

Inspired by Lutz Finger's AI Solutions class, we realized that the "Manual Computer Workload" was the primary driver of physician burnout. We teamed up with a Raytheon Mechanical Engineer to build a military-grade ingestion layer for healthcare data.

Today, we use off-the-shelf smart glasses (Meta/Vuzix) as a wedge to capture the proprietary multimodal data needed to automate clinical documentation and, eventually, diagnostics.

Clinical Workflow OS

Moving beyond simple scribing to a full operating system for surgical handoffs and point-of-care operations.

Strategic Hardware Moat

By reverse engineering the Vuzix hardware, we gain full control over both hardware and software layers. This strategic approach ensures complete data sovereignty — no third-party dependencies, no opaque black boxes, and no vendor lock-in. We control the entire stack from sensor capture to AI processing to EHR integration.

Proprietary Data Moat

Capturing RGB and audio data at critical transition points to build the world's largest dataset for automated diagnostics.
